About AMVIK

AMVIK is a cutting-edge transcription tool developed by Taarini, combining automation and human expertise to deliver accurate and efficient transcription, data filtration, proofreading, and client collaboration solutions.

  • Multiple transcription formats supported
  • Built-in data filtration & proofreading
  • Integrates with open-source transcription platforms
  • Secure client portal for project management
  • Fully customizable as per client needs
